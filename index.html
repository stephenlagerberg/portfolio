Bridging Human Cognition and Flight Automation

I am Stephen Lagerberg, specializing in the intersection of aviation psychology, human-machine teaming, and next-generation flight deck design. My focus is on optimizing pilot performance, managing cognitive workload, and ensuring that advancing AI systems serve to elevate the human operator, rather than replace them.

Core Competencies

  • Cognitive Workload Analysis: Evaluating pilot attention distribution and task saturation during high-tempo operations.
  • Human-AI Teaming: Designing intuitive interfaces for collaborative decision-making between flight crews and autonomous systems.
  • Crew Resource Management (CRM): Enhancing communication protocols in mixed human-synthetic crew environments.
  • Flight Deck Ergonomics: Applying user-centered design principles to avionics and glass cockpit displays.
  • Error Mitigation Strategy: Identifying points of systemic friction to reduce the likelihood of spatial disorientation and mode confusion.

Research & Applied Strategy

Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Simulation Analysis
