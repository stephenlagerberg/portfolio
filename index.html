About Dr. Stephen Lagerberg

I am a performance psychology consultant, aviation manager, and academic professional dedicated to elevating human potential. With a Ph.D. in General Psychology from Grand Canyon University, my research and practice focus on how individuals and teams navigate stress, maintain situational awareness, and lead effectively.

My career is rooted in high-stakes environments. From serving as a Flight Planning Chief in the United States Navy to my current role managing air traffic operations at McAllen International Airport, I understand that human performance is the most critical variable in any complex system.

As an Adjunct Professor, I teach the next generation of leaders in cognitive neuroscience, experimental psychology, and the psychology of coaching. I bring this multifaceted, evidence-based approach directly to my consulting clients, providing strategies that are rigorously tested and immediately actionable.
