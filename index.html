About Stephen

Drawing on a foundation in military operations, civilian Air Traffic Control, and advanced psychological research, Mental Altitude provides strategic consulting to high-stakes aviation environments. By combining a deep understanding of cognitive load with modern technological frameworks—backed by credentials including a Ph.D. and a Google AI Essentials certification—we design systems that protect the human operator while maximizing operational safety.

Consulting Pillars

Cognitive Load Management

Redesigning workflows and optimizing shift schedules to prevent mental overload, fatigue, and burnout in high-stress control environments.

Psychological Safety & Policy

Reforming organizational reporting structures and medical clearance policies to build a culture where highly trained staff can safely address physical and mental health vulnerabilities.

Aviation AI Integration

Leveraging artificial intelligence to relieve cognitive burdens. We advise on the deployment of predictive scheduling, dynamic workload balancing, and intelligent training simulators.
