About My Practice

I apply a Ph.D. in psychology alongside operational experience in the military and air traffic control to solve critical human-machine interface challenges. As artificial intelligence and automated decision support tools become standard in aviation, understanding how human operators process information, manage cognitive load, and make decisions under stress is vital.


My work at Mental Altitude ensures that new aerospace technologies are designed around human capabilities rather than forcing operators to adapt to rigid systems. I provide remote consulting services to organizations looking to build safer, more intuitive operational environments.

Consulting Services

Cognitive Workload Assessments

Evaluating human performance and stress responses during the integration of new flight deck automation and air traffic control decision support tools.

AI Automation Bias Evaluation

User Experience (UX) Research

Conducting specialized usability testing and human-centered design evaluations for aviation interfaces to ensure intuitive, accessible, and safe operations.
